About Us

Rekindled is an independently run Studio that specializes in full character and animation pipelines.

The studio is made up of industry experts in the fields of Character Art, Animation and Technical Animation for games and other entertainment media.

Utilizing over 10 years experience in AAA and Indie, our departments can provide high quality character modelling, fully optimised meshes with correct UV's, Texturing and shader work, Rigging, Skinning and Scripting, First and Third Person Handkeyed Animation, and Motion Capture performance and clean up.

We pride ourselves on the ability to integrate seamlessly as Co-Development partners, adapting to project, team and engine requirements
